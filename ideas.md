Concept Options - 

A — Sigil / Sacred Geometry
8 nodes arranged in a circle (like points on an octagram). Each node pulses with a glow proportional to its current sensor value. When a node crosses threshold it "activates" — its line to the centre solidifies, changes colour, starts glowing. When enough nodes activate simultaneously, the whole sigil "ignites" and collection starts. Very esoteric, ritual feel.

B — Organic Cell / Mycelium
A central cell or spore at the centre, with 8 tendril-like filaments reaching outward — one per sensor. Each tendril is alive: it wiggles gently at idle, and grows/brightens as its sensor value rises. When enough tendrils are "full", the cell triggers. Organic, biomorphic.

C — Constellation / Star Map
8 faint stars drifting slowly, connected by dim lines to a central point. When a sensor activates its star brightens and its line becomes a beam. Full alignment = all beams converge and a flash occurs. Celestial/cosmic feel.

D — Alchemical Orrery
Concentric rings orbiting a centre. Each sensor maps to a ring; a marker on each ring advances as the value rises toward threshold. When a marker completes its arc (value ≥ threshold), it locks in place and glows. When enough are locked, the orrery "opens". Mechanical but mystical.

E — Breath / Portal
A single glowing orb at centre that breathes (scales slowly). The 8 sensors manifest as petals/lobes that unfurl around it. Each petal opens proportionally to its sensor value. When enough petals are open, the portal opens and the orb expands into the collection state. Very fluid and minimal.

Concept Options - READING (FINAL)

A — Aura Field / Chromatic Bloom
A soft glowing orb at centre that expands and shifts colour in real time as sensor data comes in. Each of the 8 sensors contributes a petal or lobe of colour radiating outward — like a living aura photograph. The colours blend organically, the whole field breathes and pulses. As more data accumulates the image becomes richer and more defined, like a slow exposure developing. Very intuitive — people instantly read it as their energy.

B — Sigil Writing / Automatic Drawing
Continuing from the waiting sigil — as data comes in, lines are drawn in real time, tracing a unique geometric pattern. Each sensor controls the angle, weight or curve of a different line. The result is a one-of-a-kind sigil being "channelled" in front of the viewer. No two are the same. Feels like the machine is writing something about you.

C — Interference Patterns / Cymatics
Standing waves drawn as concentric rings that distort and interact with each other — one ring system per sensor. Where high-value sensors overlap the pattern intensifies. Looks like a Chladni figure or water surface vibration. Scientific but deeply mysterious.

D — Elemental Particles / Spirit Dust
Thousands of small particles orbiting a central point, drawn toward or repelled by the 8 sensor "poles" positioned around the canvas. The particle cloud morphs into different shapes depending on which sensors are active. Feels alive and responsive — the person's presence literally shapes the field.

E — Layered Rings / Tree Rings
Each frame of collected data adds one concentric ring to a growing circular form — like tree rings or a cross-section of something organic. The ring's colour, thickness and texture are determined by the sensor balance. Over the collection period a complete circular "record" of the person builds up. Meditative and accumulative.

F — Neural Constellation
8 nodes (same as the sigil) connected by lines whose thickness and brightness pulse with the live sensor values. The constellation trembles and shifts, like a nervous system firing. Connections between active nodes glow brighter. The whole thing feels like a brain scan or energy map being charted.

My Recommendation
A + B together would be exceptional for this installation:
